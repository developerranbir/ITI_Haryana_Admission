Rules & Regulations

Access & Eligibility

1
Access is available at any Government ITI facility convenient to the user upon registration and payment verification.
2
A digitally generated access pass must be obtained prior to visiting any ITI library or workshop facility.
3
Passes are generated exclusively after successful prepaid payment or confirmed advance booking of available slots.
4
All users must carry valid government-issued photo identification along with their access pass during facility visits.
5
Facility capacity is limited to 20 users simultaneously to ensure optimal learning environment and safety compliance.

Financial Policies

6
Library sitting access is available at ₹100 per month, providing reading area privileges without book issuance rights.
7
Comprehensive library access including book issuance privileges is available at ₹500 per monthly cycle.
8
Workshop training sessions are billed at ₹300 per instructional hour, with material costs billed separately.
9
Maximum daily workshop participation is limited to 4 instructional hours to prevent fatigue and ensure safety.

Conduct & Compliance

10
All users must maintain professional decorum and adhere to established disciplinary standards within facility premises.
11
Access privileges will be immediately revoked for any disciplinary violations or misconduct without refund.
12
Mobile devices must be switched to silent mode; calls should be taken only in designated areas outside learning spaces.
13
Consumption of food, beverages, and tobacco products is strictly prohibited within library and workshop areas.
14
Users are financially responsible for any damage to equipment, books, or facilities resulting from improper handling.

Safety & Operations

15
All workshop participants must complete safety orientation before equipment access and wear appropriate protective gear.
16
Reservations for library seating and workshop equipment are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
17
Borrowed materials must be returned by the due date; overdue items will incur penalties of ₹10 per day.
18
The administration reserves the right to modify operational policies, schedules, and fees with prior notice.
19
Personal belongings should be stored in designated lockers; the facility assumes no liability for lost or stolen items.
